Comparative Research for ISEA 2017

Needfindings

The audience are able to understand the storyboard clearly, and they can learn how the application is working. Based on the storyboard, they can also learn when and where to use the application, in other words, understand the application's purpose and features.

The environment of the storyboard takes place at the super market. Customer has trouble choosing which product to buy because both products are the same and have same price. Therefore, the customer uses my scanner app to look up each product's sustainability in order to make a better decision.
